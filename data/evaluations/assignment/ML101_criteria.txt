Evaluation Criteria for Assignments:

Concept Understanding – 25
Shows grasp of Locally Weighted Regression and weight logic.

Implementation – 25
Correct dataset generation and LWR algorithm code.

Experimentation – 20
Tests multiple query points and bandwidths with analysis.

Visualization – 15
Clear, labeled plots comparing fits.

Presentation – 15
Well-structured PDF with code, results, and explanations.

Higher weight questions should demonstrate deeper understanding and application.
Lower weight questions focus on basic concept correctness.
